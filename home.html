01

How do you measure marketing ROI?

+

I move beyond vanity metrics like "likes." My focus is on Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Lifetime Value (LTV), and ROAS. We set clear KPIs before launching any campaign to ensure every dollar spent contributes to tangible revenue growth.

02

What is the timeline for SEO results?

+

SEO is a long-term compound asset. While technical fixes can show impact in 2-4 weeks, significant organic traffic growth typically stabilizes between 3 to 6 months. I provide monthly data visualization reports so you can see the trajectory clearly.

03

Do you handle Creative or just Strategy?

+

I bridge the gap. As a Full-Stack Marketer, I handle the strategy, technical setup, and ad copywriting. for high-end video production or complex graphic design, I direct a network of trusted specialists to ensure the visual quality matches the strategic depth.

04

What makes your approach "Data-Driven"?

+

I don't guess. I use tools like GA4, Semrush, and Looker Studio to analyze user behavior. My campaigns utilize A/B testing protocols to scientifically determine which headlines, creatives, and audiences perform best, minimizing budget waste.

05

Do you work with Startups or Enterprises?

+

My framework is scalable. I have extensive experience with early-stage startups (like Dotco) focusing on aggressive growth hacking, as well as established brands (like Kindled) focusing on brand reputation and community engagement.
